Why Your Body Needs Exercise

Your body is like a supercar. The more you use it,the stronger it gets! Kids who move for 60 minutes everydayare happier, sleep better and do better in school.

Dancing, swimming,yoga and anything that gets your heart beating faster counts. Keep moving and have fun!

quick health tips

  1. Eat 5 fruits and veggies a day
  2. Drink 8 cups of water daily
  3. sleep 9 to 11 hours every night so that you have enough energy for the day
  4. Move your body for 60 minutes everyday
