Itadori Yuji

Itadori Yuji is the main protagonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High and a member of the Jujutsu Sorcerers. He is known for his incredible physical abilities and his kind-hearted nature.

Personality

Yuji is known for his cheerful and optimistic personality. He values friendship and is always willing to help others, even at great personal risk. His determination and courage make him a beloved character among fans of the series.

Abilities

Yuji possesses superhuman strength, speed, and agility. He is also skilled in hand-to-hand combat and has the ability to use cursed energy to enhance his attacks. His most notable ability is his connection to Sukuna, the King of Curses, which grants him access to powerful techniques.
